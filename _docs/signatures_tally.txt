 not necessarily always be 100% precise.

 by phpMussel at this time.

 Although in heavily modified and reduced form,
 phpMussel uses the PhishTank CSV of known phishing
 URLs to populate the main bulk of its standard mail
 scanning signatures.

 SecuriteInfo.com provides a range of good optional
 supplementary MD5 signatures for the ClamAV engine.
 PhpMussel adapts a selection of those for within its
 "complex extended" signatures functionality.
